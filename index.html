About Me

Cultural Consultant & Brand Specialist

I’m a writer and storyteller based in Tokyo. Ever since I was a child, I had a genuine curiosity about the world. I was that kid who couldn’t stop asking “why?”—much to my dad’s amusement (and occasional exhaustion). But for a long time, I didn’t see curiosity as something to be proud of. There’s a saying in Japan: The nail that sticks out gets hammered down. Growing up, I often felt the pressure to fit in, so I learned to quiet my questions. But deep down, I knew that asking questions and connecting with others brought me so much joy. Over time, my curiosity led me to teach myself English, connect with people from different cultures, and explore over 16 countries.

Eventually, I realized that my curiosity wasn’t a flaw—it was my superpower. It’s how I make sense of the world and help others do the same.
